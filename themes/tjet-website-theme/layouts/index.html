Collect, analyze, and share data from your hardware. Code not required.

TelemetryJet empowers teams to gain insight from their data.

TelemetryJet is a no-code platform for time series data collection, analysis, and sharing. The TelemetryJet platform provides a set of powerful tools and integrations, allowing your team to spend less time wrangling your data and more time utilizing it productively.

Collect and aggregate data, regardless of the source.

TelemetryJet can ingest data from a wide range of industry-standard formats. Easily upload data manually, or use the open-source TelemetryJet local server to stream data as it occurs.

Analyze and visualize your data from anywhere.

Quickly build charts and dashboards that are accurate, easy to interpret, and beautiful. TelemetryJet uses intelligent streaming and filtering that allows you to view and analyze large datasets in the web browser.

Share your data in an instant.

TelemetryJet is built with sharing in mind: Data, visualizations, and dashboards can instantly be shared with anyone.

Our team plans allow collaboration on the platform among multiple users, and all visualizations can be shared through a public link with anyone in your community.

You retain ownership of your data, and can easily export data out of the TelemetryJet platform into standard formats for advanced usage.
