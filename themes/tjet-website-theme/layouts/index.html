The ultimate data collection and visualization platform for makers.

It's easier than ever to interact with hardware projects, small or large.

Stream time-series data from your project, with an easy-to-use set of libraries and integrations. TelemetryJet can collect and store data from a wide range of protocols and systems, ranging from a simple .csv file to a complex ROS-powered system.

View, record, and analyze data.

TelemetryJet is the first step to building an intelligent system. TelemetryJet takes care of the collection and storage of data, allowing you to focus on gaining insight from that data. Plot your data, compare results, and perform statistical analysis using the UI’s charting and analysis features.

Build beautiful, functional dashboards...

And share them from anywhere.

TelemetryJet features a rich, flexible dashboard editor with a large component library. You can easily lay out complex dashboards and data visualizations. Once you’re done, use the TelemetryJet Cloud to share live dashboards with your team, anywhere they are.
